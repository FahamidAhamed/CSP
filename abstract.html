ABSTRACT


 The identification of social media users is essential for targeted advertising, personalized recommendations, and social research. This paper explores various methods, from traditional user profiles to advanced algorithms using machine learning. Ethical considerations, including privacy and algorithmic biases, highlight the need for responsible practices in user identification.
